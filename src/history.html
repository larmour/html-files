History

Nike, Inc. is an American multinational corporation that designs, develops, manufactures and sells several products. Some of Nike’s products include footwear, apparel, equipment and accessories. The company’s headquarter is in Beaverton, Oregon. Nike is one of the largest suppliers of athletic shoes and apparel worldwide

The company was founded as Blue Ribbon Sports by University of Oregon coach Bill Bowerman and athlete Phil Knight on January 25, 1964. The company started off as a distributor for Japanese shoe maker. The name became Nike, Inc. on May 30, 1971. The company got the name from the Greek goddess of victory.

As far as Nike's finances the revenues for the brand were $7.7 billion in the fourth quarter for 2016. That is an 8% increase on a currency-neutral level. The gross margin decline to 45.9%. The overall net income was $58 million.
